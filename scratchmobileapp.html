Instructions on How to Play

Standard Play: Quick Start

  1. 2-4 players
    • Ie. John
    • Jimmy
    • Sally
    • Frank
  2. Sit on a chair in an equally spaced circle
  3. Each player gets a lasso
  4. Place the Jacks in the circle
  5. Count down from 3 and everyone yells, "YeeHaw!" and begins trying to lasso the jacks
  6. Place Lasso jacks in front of your legs
  7. You may lasso jacks from the circle or from in front of other players
  8. With 4 players, the first to collect 4 jacks wins

Setup

  1. Reference the diagram below
  2. Players sit in a circle 10'-15' across
  3. Spread the Jacks in the circle (the open range)
  4. Area in front of legs is your stable (captured jack zone)

Objective

Be the first to capture the winning number of jacks with your lasso.

  • 4 Players: 4 Jacks
  • 3 Players: 5 Jacks
  • 2 Players: 6 Jacks

How to Lasso

Makin' the Lasso Loop

  1. Feed the tail through the honda loop until your loop is double the target size

How to Hold the Lasso

  1. Hold the honda loop with your non-dominant hand
  2. Slide your dominant hand down about 12 inches, grabbing both loop and slack
  3. Let go with non-dominant hand
  4. Grab the tail end with non-dominant hand again to manage slack

Swinging the Lasso

  1. Start swinging the loop overhead
  2. Wrist should rotate to open the loop

Aim, Release, and Follow Through

  1. Aim just top-left of the target
  2. Release with a forward skipping motion
  3. Point to guide the loop
  4. Pull tail end to cinch once jack is caught
